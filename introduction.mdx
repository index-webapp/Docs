## Get Up and Running

You can start exploring SURCHI without an account. Here's the fastest path from zero to insight:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Wallet">
    Visit [surchi.xyz](https://surchi.xyz) and connect your wallet using any EVM-compatible wallet or Solana wallet (e.g., Phantom). Connecting unlocks personalized watchlists, saved searches, and alert notifications. Browsing is always available without a wallet connection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore Tokens">
    Enter a contract address, token name, or ticker in the search bar to pull up a full token profile — live price, liquidity depth, holder distribution, transaction history, and AI risk score. Switch between chains using the chain selector in the top navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze & Track">
    Run an AI audit on any contract, paste a wallet address to see full portfolio analytics, or configure price and on-chain alerts to stay ahead of market movements. Everything updates in real time via WebSocket connections.
  </Step>
</Steps>
