## Risk Warning

Engaging with cryptocurrencies, decentralized finance (DeFi) protocols, and blockchain-based assets involves a unique and substantial set of risks that you should fully understand before participating. The following is a non-exhaustive list of the key risk categories relevant to SURCHI users.

**Market Volatility Risk**
Cryptocurrency prices are extremely volatile. Token values can increase or decrease by large percentages within minutes, hours, or days. Market conditions can change suddenly and without warning due to macroeconomic events, regulatory announcements, social media activity, or the actions of large market participants. You may lose a substantial portion — or all — of the value of your investment at any time.

**Smart Contract Risk**
Even tokens that receive a low risk score from SURCHI's AI Audit may contain undiscovered vulnerabilities in their smart contract code. No automated or manual audit can guarantee the absence of all bugs, exploits, or malicious logic. Smart contract exploits can result in the total loss of funds held in or associated with the affected contract. SURCHI's audit scores reflect the current state of our detection models and known vulnerability patterns — they are not a warranty of contract safety.

**Liquidity Risk**
Many tokens on decentralized exchanges have shallow liquidity pools, meaning that large trades can cause significant price impact (slippage). In extreme cases, you may be unable to sell a token at any meaningful price if liquidity is removed or if the token becomes worthless. Liquidity lock mechanisms reduce but do not eliminate this risk.

**Regulatory Risk**
The legal status of cryptocurrencies and DeFi protocols varies significantly by jurisdiction and is subject to rapid change. Regulatory actions — including exchange bans, asset seizures, or requirements for identity verification — could materially impact the value or usability of digital assets. Users are responsible for understanding and complying with the laws and regulations in their own jurisdiction.

**Counterparty and Protocol Risk**
Third-party services, DEXs, bridges, and protocols that SURCHI references or integrates with carry their own risks, including smart contract vulnerabilities, team misconduct, and operational failures. SURCHI does not control these third-party systems and cannot be responsible for losses arising from their failure.

**Past performance is not indicative of future results.** Historical price data, wallet PnL records, and AI model accuracy statistics shown on the platform are retrospective and do not guarantee similar outcomes in the future.

<Warning>
  **You may lose the entire value of your investment.** Do not invest more than you are prepared to lose completely. AI audit scores, wallet intelligence data, and any other information provided by SURCHI do not constitute a guarantee of token safety or investment suitability.
