## Platform Access Tiers

Your $SURCHI balance determines which tier of the platform you can access. Tiers are checked in real time against your connected wallet — no staking or locking is required to qualify. Simply hold the tokens in your wallet.

| Tier | $SURCHI Required | Features Unlocked |
|---|---|---|
| Free | 0 | Basic explorer, public token data |
| Standard | 1,000 | Full wallet intelligence, AI audits |
| Pro | 10,000 | API access, webhooks, advanced charts |
| Elite | 100,000 | Priority API, dedicated support, early features |

Tier upgrades take effect immediately once your wallet balance crosses the threshold, and downgrades apply at the next session check if your balance falls below a tier's requirement.

## Governance Rights

Every $SURCHI holder is a stakeholder in the platform. Token-weighted governance gives you a direct vote on proposals that shape the future of SURCHI, including:

- **Product roadmap priorities** — which new chains, features, or integrations get built next
- **Ecosystem fund allocation** — how the 10% ecosystem treasury is spent
- **Fee structures** — API pricing, tier thresholds, and partner revenue sharing
- **Protocol changes** — updates to the governance process itself

Voting power is proportional to your $SURCHI balance at the time a snapshot is taken for each proposal. You do not need to delegate or lock tokens to vote — holding in your wallet is sufficient. Proposals can be submitted by any wallet meeting the minimum proposal threshold, ensuring governance remains open and permissionless.

Learn how to participate in the [Governance](/community/governance) section.

## Staking & Rewards

A $SURCHI staking programme is on the roadmap, designed to reward long-term holders and liquidity providers who contribute to the health of the ecosystem.

**Planned staking mechanics include:**

- **Liquidity staking** — stake your Raydium or Orca LP tokens to earn $SURCHI rewards distributed from the ecosystem allocation
- **Single-sided staking** — lock $SURCHI for a defined period and earn yield funded by platform revenue sharing
- **Reward boosts** — higher tier holders who stake receive multiplied reward rates
- **Governance weight boost** — staked tokens may count with an additional multiplier in governance votes, rewarding committed long-term participants

Staking launch details, APY ranges, and lock-up options will be announced through official SURCHI channels. The staking contract will be publicly audited before deployment.

The role of $SURCHI is designed to grow alongside the platform. Several major expansions are planned that will deepen the utility of the token:

- **SURCHI DAO** — a fully on-chain decentralised autonomous organisation that gives $SURCHI holders binding governance authority over the protocol treasury, upgrade keys, and fee parameters

- **Premium data marketplace** — a peer-to-peer marketplace where analysts can publish and sell curated on-chain datasets, strategy scripts, and alert configurations, with $SURCHI as the settlement currency
- **Partner protocol integrations** — joint tier programmes with DeFi protocols that reward $SURCHI holders with preferential rates, boosted yields, or early access to new product features
