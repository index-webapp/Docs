$SURCHI is the native utility token of the SURCHI multi-chain analytics platform. Built as a Solana SPL token, $SURCHI gives you access to premium analytics features, governance rights, and the broader SURCHI ecosystem. With a fixed total supply of 19,897,905 tokens and a permanently revoked mint authority, $SURCHI is designed to be a transparent, community-owned asset with no possibility of inflation or hidden dilution.

## Token Details

The table below summarises the core on-chain properties of the $SURCHI token.

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Name | SURCHI |

| Blockchain | Solana |
| Token Standard | SPL |
| Total Supply | 19,897,905 |
| Decimals | 9 |
| Mint Authority | Revoked |
| Freeze Authority | None |

## Key Properties

### Fixed Supply — Forever

The mint authority for $SURCHI was revoked immediately after the token launch. In practical terms, this means:

- **No new tokens can ever be created.** The total supply of 19,897,905 $SURCHI is the absolute maximum that will ever exist.
- **No inflation.** There is no mechanism — technical or otherwise — that allows any party, including the SURCHI team, to mint additional tokens.
- **Your share of the supply is protected.** Because no new tokens can be introduced, holding $SURCHI means holding a known, immutable percentage of the total supply.

The absence of a freeze authority provides a further guarantee: no wallet holding $SURCHI can have its tokens frozen or confiscated at the token-contract level.

Holding $SURCHI unlocks tiered access to the most powerful features on the platform. ## Governance

$SURCHI holders have a direct voice in the future of the platform. Token-weighted governance voting allows the community to propose and vote on product roadmap priorities, fee structures, ecosystem fund allocation, and protocol-level changes. Governance participation is open to any wallet holding $SURCHI above the applicable proposal or voting threshold. Learn more on the [Governance](/community/governance) page.
